I decide to stop her and ask for her name.
I feel guilty just calling her 'maid-san'.
<Page2>
"Excuse me, may I ask your name?
I just got here, so I haven't really grasped the
situation yet."
I call out to her.
The girl in the kimono looks at me, eyes wide.
<Page3>
"My name is Kohaku. In this mansion, I serve the head
of the Tohno family."
<Page4>
Smiling, the girl bows after her introduction.
It was a gentle motion, like that of a flower blooming
from a bud.
