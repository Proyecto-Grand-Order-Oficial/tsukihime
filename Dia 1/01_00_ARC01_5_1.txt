<Page1>
I obediently follow her.
I may have lived in this mansion seven years ago, but I
was a child then. Although I remember some parts of it,
my memory is too vague to make sense of it.
I take a single deep breath and step into my nostalgic
family home.
<Page2>
And then...
"Oh dear. I've gone and done things out of order."
Whether she knows I'm nervous or not, the girl turns
around and bows politely.
<Page3>
"Welcome home, Shiki-sama.
I look forward to working for you."
...The smile she greets me with―――it really does
remind me of a blossom.
Unable to come up with an appropriate reply, I timidly
follow her.
